''Das LockOffice ist der erste Interims-CoWorkingSpace der LockSchuppenAg auf der LouisenStrasse in der NeuStadt der StadtDresden (LockOfficeAnfahrt).''

The LockOffice is the first interim CoWorkingSpace of LockSchuppenAg at LouisenStrasse in the NeuStadt of the CityOfDresden.

The LockOffice is THE connecting hub, built as a creative CoWorkingSpace, where EntrePreneur's, DeSign'ers, ProGram'mers, ReSearch'er and other people interested in collaborative ways of working can meet.

We offer CoWorkingSpace for EntrePreneur's and FreeLancer on hourly, daily and monthly basis.

At the ZukunftsRaum we operate the FutureLab2056 where we develop LongTerm scenarios for the TwentyFirstCentury, which will be the StartingPoint of the future SingularAcademy.

Our ~25 sqm LockAgora is a facility for WorkShop's and CoLearning (web and IT tools making collaborative work more effective and relaxing).
