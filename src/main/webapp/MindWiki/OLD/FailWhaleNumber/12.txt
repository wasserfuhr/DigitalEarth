The FailWhaleNumber is a little DegreesOfSeparation game similar to the ErdosNumber, BaconNumber or TurkNumber:

Find your DegreesOfSeparation from JackDorsey:

The FailWhale number is defined as follows:
* JackDorsey, CEO and MindOne of TwittEr has FailWhaleNumber 0
* the __first 36 followings__ of JackDorsey have FailWhaleNumber 1 (TwittEr shows exactly the 36 first followings on a profile page)
* the __first 36 followings__ of somebody with FailWhaleNumber 1 have FailWhaleNumber 2
* etc etc.
