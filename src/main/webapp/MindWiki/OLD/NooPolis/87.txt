NooPolis is a game about a self-organizing WikiBased EmergentDemocracy which simulates a VirtualEconomy inside the NooSphere.

Players of NooPolis
* form a SocialNetwork of CitiZens, based on a WebOfTrust and mutual responsibility 
* create, discuss and evolve the ConStitution for one of the simplest and most TransParent virtual economic, legal and political systems on PlanetEarth,
* discuss and decide the GovernmentBudget,
* elect the CabiNet at the CabiNetElection, 
* have a BankAccount for receiving and transfering of the virtual currency KayGroschen
* create virtual products and offer them in the MindShop,
* found StartUp's which can be traded on the MindFloor,
* understand, discuss and shape the AcCelerate'ing changes in science, technology and global markets by actively defining ForeSight scenarios,
* playfully mashup people and places from RealLife with fictitious, but in principle possible locations, events and trends on the FutureMap,
* create futuristic KunstWerk's,
* improve the LifeExpectancy of all CitiZens and respect their LastWill.
