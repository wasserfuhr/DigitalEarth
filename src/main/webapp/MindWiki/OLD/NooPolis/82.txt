NooPolis is a web- and WikiBased multiuser game which simulates a VirtualEconomy and a self-organizing EmergentDemocracy.

Players of NooPolis can
* create and participate in a VirtualEconomy for inventing, developing and trading products and services,
** have a BankAccount, 
** can found StartUp's which can be traded on the MindFloor,
* elect the government CabiNet at CabiNetElection's,
* discuss and evolve the ConStitution for one of the simplest and most TransParent virtual economic, legal and political systems on PlanetEarth,
* understand, discuss and shape the AcCelerate'ing changes in science, technology and global markets by actively defining ForeSight scenarios,
* playfully mashup people and places from RealLife with fictitious, but in principle possible locations, events and trends in the future,
* create futuristic KunstWerk's,
* improve the life expectancy of all CitiZens and respect their LastWill.
